About Us

Pikine was founded in 2015 by Amadou Diallo. Pikine is a Senegalese Restaurant that serves authentic Senegalese cuisine. Our menu features traditional dishes such as Thieboudienne, Yassa, and Maffe. We pride ourselves on delivering fresh, flavorful dishes in a warm and welcoming environment. We are open daily from 10 AM to 10 PM. Visit us today to experience the rich flavors of Senegal.
